User Guide: Weather App

    Step 1: Location Selection
  • Launch the Weather App.
  • Locate the search box and input your desired city's name
    Step 2: Weather Retrieval
  • Once the city is entered, hit the "Get Weather" button to initiate the data fetch.
    Step 3: Weather Insights
  • After a moment, you'll be directed to the results page, where you can find:
  • Current temperature.
  • Detailed weather description.
    Step 4: Plan with Confidence
  • Armed with this data, plan your activities according to the provided weather forecast.
